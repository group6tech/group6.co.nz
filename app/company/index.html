Meet Group 6, your broadcast and post production facility partner

{% img_srcset /images/content/company/james.jpg, James at SMPTE %}

Group 6 Technologies was formed in 2003 by James Gardner and Matt Dwen, both graduates of South Seas Film & Television School.

Their television and IT industry experience led to the development of DIVA, a media sharing tool for editors by editors. DIVA is now the most cost-effective distributed media management tool available in the market today.

{% img_srcset /images/content/company/Matt.jpg, Matt at Cream Media %}

Since then, Group 6 has gone on to design and build a number of post production and broadcast facilities. The company has also developed a number of unique systems and industry innovations, as well as providing IT and telco support.

Group 6 is proud to include Eyeworks New Zealand, Imagination Television, South Pacific Pictures, Screentime Productions, Greenstone TV, Juice TV and others as clients.
